# SaaS Revenue & Churn Analytics Dashboard

Two-page Power BI dashboard built on a sample SaaS dataset (RavenStack) to show how I approach subscription analytics, retention, and churn diagnostics.

## Contents

- `dashboard/RavenStack_SaaS_Revenue_Churn_Dashboard.pdf`  
  Export of the full Power BI report (2 pages).

- `data/` Sample SaaS source tables in CSV format (`ravenstack_accounts`, `ravenstack_subscriptions`,
  `ravenstack_churn_events`, `ravenstack_feature_usage`, `ravenstack_support_tickets`).

## Pages

1. **Revenue & Subscription Analytics**  
   - ARR, revenue per active subscription  
   - Active subscriptions & accounts  
   - ARR by tier and industry  
   - Subscription counts by tier and industry  

2. **Cohort & Churn Analytics**  
   - Retention curve by account age  
   - Cohort retention heatmap (signup month Ã— age in months)  
   - Churn rate by industry  
   - Top churn reasons (last 12 months)

> This is a portfolio project using a synthetic / sample dataset, not real customer data.
